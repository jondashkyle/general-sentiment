Control how platforms appear to you. Your Fence lets in what you want in, and keeps out what you want out.

Blocker apps limit your access to adversarial platforms, but are easily overcome. Fences promote adaptation over rejection. A Fence lets you selectively remove parts of platforms not in tune with your values.

You do this by using your agent of choice to describe the changes you want to see to the platforms you visit.

Find yourself clicking around related videos on Youtube instead of ending your session after watching the one you intended to? Tell an agent to modify your fence to hide related videos on Youtube.

Find Reels distracting on Instagram? Tell an agent to modify your fence to remove them from the interface. While you’re at it, when visiting the feed redirect you to the linear feed instead of the algorithmic one.
