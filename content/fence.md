Lets you control how platforms appear to you. Your Fence lets in what you want in, and keeps out what you want out.

Blocker apps limit your access to distractions and platforms, but are easily overcome. Rejection instead of adaptation. A fence lets you selectively remove parts of platforms not in tune with your values.

History does not follow reason. It’s messy. Thanks to freakish luck, many platforms have a browser client. And in the browser you can fuck things up. You can get your hands on things. The platforms may not want you to, but there are ways.

> “I think you owe it to yourself to take some time today and start building your tech fence” — [Pewdiepie](https://youtu.be/5nL-Eq1lpDU)
